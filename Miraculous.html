MIRACULOUS

VR LABYRINTH

For this project, I led a group of interns made up of three programmers, one technical artist, and one 3D artist. Over the course of three months, we developed a virtual reality game set in the universe of Miraculous, designed to be played in a 3x3 meter space by children between the ages of approximately 6 and 12. The game consists of a series of balance tests, shooting mechanics at targets with a slingshot, climbing ladders, ascending in elevators, and a final battle against a blimp at the top of the Eiffel tower.

Some of my responsabilities included:

  • Leading the project
  • Being the lead programmer
  • Supervising and reviewing design documents
