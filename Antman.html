ANT-MAN & THE WASP

IN THE HIVE

HACKING MINIGAMES

I developed a series of mini-games focused on "hacking" that are designed to be played on touch screens for an escape room experience inspired by Antman and the Wasp. One player acts as the master, controlling the game from a desktop computer using a keyboard and mouse, and has access to information about the solutions to the mini-games. On three separate touch screens placed on the wall, three other players must solve the mini-games simultaneously. If one player makes a mistake, it results in a penalty for the rest of the players. For this project, I worked closely with the design team to ensure that their ideas were accurately reflected in the final product. As a 2D game, the main focus was on creating touch-controlled interfaces. We aimed to provide an appropriate pace and feedback to give the player the feeling of hacking against the clock.
